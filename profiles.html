Meet the Team

We are the students that form Thirty Seven Gang, collaborating together to present our first group assignment for the second assessment for Introduction to Information Technology at RMIT. Each member will have their own intimate profile that summarises part of their background, their interest in IT and their individual personality profiles. Under the Team Profiles dropdown menu in the navigation bar just above, there is a subsection called Ideal Jobs where we contrast and compare our ideal careers to where we are at today, where we want to be tomorrow and how we need to go about it in order to progress on our career journeys.

Coming up with a team name required a bit of collective input and the suggestion on incorporating the group number we were assigned by our lecturers felt like the right thing to do and we declared ourselves the Thirty Seven Gang! You can view this meeting where we decide our team name in the Tools section of our website.
