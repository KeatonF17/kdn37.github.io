Overview

As a group, the members of Thirty Seven Gang elected to choose four technologies to present a report on. The technologies chosen represent individual choice, interest and the way they're rapidly evolving within the indsutry. Due to his interest in this sector of the industry, Nelson chose to do Blockchain & Cryptocurrencies because of the way it's revolutionising the way business is conducted and how personal wealth can be managed. Keaton chose to do Cyber Security **becase*. Dean chose to do Machine Learning **because**, and Sean chose to do Robotics **because**.
