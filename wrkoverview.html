Thirty Seven Gang approached this topic with a bit more detail than requested, given the geographic difference by most members, it was agreed that the best approach was to summarise on multiple accounts what and IT professional does, who they interact with, where they spend most of their time and what they find the most challenging in their role - that can be found here or by navigating the drop-down menu.
