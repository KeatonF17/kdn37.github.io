Overview

The IT industry is large, varied and changes over time. It also easy to get a misleading impression of what is typically involved on a day-to-day basis from a job advertisement or a position description. In order to help you understand this, the best way seems to be to hear from some IT professionals about their daily work. There are two ways in which you can do this, as below.

As the IT industry is an evergrowing and robust industry, the companies that operate in this field a generally specific. Some companies operate in the field of developing software and internet related products and services, to producing hardware and semiconductor equipment (Miller, 2021). https://www.valuepenguin.com/sectors/information-technology

To address our understanding of the industry at work, we decided as a group to report on the accounts by a select few IT progessionals as this provides the understanding from multiple perspectives the way the industry carries out their work. Additionaly to supplement the primary report, we have included an interview conducted with an IT professional that has many years of experience in the industry and demonstrates their contribution to their employer in a software and internet related service environment.
